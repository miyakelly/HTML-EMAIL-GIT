Dear colleague,

I’d like to share with you information about a new online platform that I think will be very useful to you and your faculty.

The platform, DIA2, is a Web tool where you can analyze and visualize the NSF funding portfolio. It is the product of a collaborative grant I have been working on.
With DIA2, you can quickly:

• Identify collaborators working on a given topic
• Examine your institution’s funding profile
• Understand the overall NSF funding portfolio
• … and much more. Please learn more at www.dia2.org
DIA2 is easy to use, and requires no expertise in data mining and visualization. It is free and you can use it today. For the best experience, please use it on your desktop computer.
I am proud of the work our team has done on DIA2, and truly believe it is a useful tool. I would appreciate your help spreading the word about it - please consider sharing this information with your faculty.
